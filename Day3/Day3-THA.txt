
To Subscribe the channel
SUBSCRIBE channel [channel_name]
To Publish to the channel
PUBLISH channel [message]
e.g. 
127.0.0.1:6379> SUBSCRIBE devsnest
127.0.0.1:6379> PUBLISH devsnest hii                                                                                        Videos
(integer) 1                                                                                                                 anaconda3        
127.0.0.1:6379> PUBLISH devsnest Hello                                a4692b}.TM.blf                                        ansel
(integer) 1                                                           a4692b}.TMContainer00000000000000000001.regtrans-ms   eclipse
127.0.0.1:6379>     

Output

Reading messages... (press Ctrl-C to quit)
1) "subscribe"
2) "devsnest"
3) (integer) 1
1) "message"
2) "devsnest"
3) "hii"
1) "message"
2) "devsnest"
3) "Hello"
